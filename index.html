Local hustlers upload their products on the Upload Page by filling in product details such as type, name, price, image, and contact information.

Step 2: Browse

Retailers visit the Browse Page to view all uploaded products in a clean and professional layout.

Step 3: Connect

Retailers search by location or city, view seller details, and connect directly through WhatsApp or call buttons.
